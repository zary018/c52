Interesting facts about Camels

    There are two types of Camels, one have one hump, and the other have two. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting facts about Chameleons

    Chameleons can change their skin color. Chameleons have eyes in the back of their heads Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues

Interesting facts about Cicadas

    Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans There are more than 3,000 species of Cicadas Cicadas can buzz louder than a lawnmower

Interesting facts about Pandas

    Pandas have great camouflage for their environment Panda eyes are different to normal bears Pandas spend 10-16 hours of there day eating!

Interesting facts about Pigeons

    Pigeons fought alongside humans in World War I and II Pigeons are very complex and intelligent animals Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
